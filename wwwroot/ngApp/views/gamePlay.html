How To Play




Controls:

Collect as many points as possible within 20 seconds!

The main controls utilized in our games are A Mouse and Arrow Keys.

Regular Games:

Games you play when you click within your radius.

Use keyboard arrows to move around and collect the items that appear.

Special Games:

Games you play when you click on an icon.

  • Dragon Breath: Use your mouse to move your dragon and left-click to first throw you fire breath.
  • JasInvaders: Use the arrow keys to move dragon and the spacebar to shoot fireballs
  • Sky Boating: Use the arrow keys to move around (Keep in mind your physics is a little off) and collect as many stars as you can
  • Dragon vs Tanks: Use your mouse to aim and left-click to shoot and arrow keys to move around
